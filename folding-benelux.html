Folding Benelux

Distributed Computing for Belgium, Netherlands & Luxembourg

Team ID: 1067639

Folding Benelux is the regional Folding@home team uniting contributors from Belgium, the Netherlands, and Luxembourg. Whether you're running a high-end GPU rig or a laptop, every Work Unit strengthens biomedical research.

Mission

Our goal is simple: create a recognizable Benelux presence on the global Folding@home leaderboard. We support scientific studies involving cancer, neurodegenerative diseases, and infectious disease research.

Join the Team

You can join Folding Benelux by adding the team ID inside your Folding@home client.
